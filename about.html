About Me

Hi, I'm Momin – a young entrepreneur with a passion for business, technology, and personal growth. I founded CustomHydration in 2025 with a clear mission: to make hydration more sustainable and personal. Our brand focuses on customizable metal and glass bottles that are both eco-friendly and stylish.

My journey into entrepreneurship started early. Last summer, I gained valuable experience by selling treats, learning how to connect with customers and manage a small-scale operation. That experience sparked a deeper interest in building something bigger – something with impact.

With CustomHydration, my goal is not just to sell products, but to help shape a low-carbon future through innovation, smart design, and purpose-driven business. This is just the beginning.
